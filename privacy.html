Privacy Protocol

Last Revised: February 2026

01. Data Ingestion

We collect essential telemetry and professional credentials provided during the application process. This data is encrypted via AES-256 standard and stored within our secure cloud silos.

02. Usage Logic

Your information is utilized exclusively for cultural and technical matching. Innovet Tech does not sell, lease, or distribute your dossier to third-party entities for marketing purposes.

03. Right to Erasure

In accordance with global digital rights standards, users may request the permanent decommissioning of their data from our servers at any time via the Security Channel.
